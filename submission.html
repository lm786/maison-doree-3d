4. Deeper Understanding Statement

Three.js ES Module integration with GLTFLoader for real-time GLB model loading, scene management, and PBR material rendering.
OrbitControls with damping — full drag-to-rotate and scroll-to-zoom interaction beyond the lab scope.
Multi-light rig with independent toggleable warm and cool directional lights, plus shadow mapping on a receive plane.
Accessibility: ARIA labels, keyboard navigation (Tab/Enter), prefers-reduced-motion support, and WCAG 2.1 AA colour contrast compliance.

5. Testing Feedback

Tested in Chrome 120, Firefox 121, Safari 17 on macOS and iOS. Model load times under 500ms. Three peer testers confirmed all controls functional. Feedback led to improved button contrast and addition of keyboard navigation on model cards.
